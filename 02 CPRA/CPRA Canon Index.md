# CPRA Canon Index

Use this page as the launchpad for the **Code of Professional Responsibility and Accountability**.

## Canon map

- [[Canon I - Independence]] — independent, accessible, efficient, and effective legal service.
- [[Canon II - Propriety]] — propriety in personal and professional conduct.
- [[Canon III - Fidelity]] — fidelity to the Constitution, law, administration of justice, and client cause within truth and law.
- [[Canon IV - Competence and Diligence]] — legal knowledge, skill, preparation, promptness, and communication.
- [[Canon V - Equality]] — equal treatment, non-discrimination, and access to justice.
- [[Canon VI - Accountability]] — disciplinary jurisdiction, procedure, sanctions, and clemency.
