# Case Digest Template

Use this template for every assigned case digest in `05 Cases/`.

## Facts

Write only the facts needed to understand the ethical issue. Avoid copying long passages from the decision.

## Issue

State the legal-ethics issue as a question.

## Ruling

State the Court's answer and the sanction or outcome, if any.

## Doctrine

Extract the rule that should be remembered.

## Ethics relevance

Explain why the case appears in the Basic Legal and Judicial Ethics syllabus.

## Bar trap

Identify the likely exam mistake.

## Syllabus topic

Link the exact syllabus topic.

Example:

- [[Practice of Law]]
- [[Admission to the Bar]]
- [[Unauthorized Practice of Law]]
- [[Canon I - Independence]]

## Related CPRA / Rule of Court provision

List the controlling provision first, then related provisions.

Example:

- CPRA, Canon I, Sec. 1
- Rule 138, Sec. 1
- Rule 138-A
